Bazaar 1.0 By Manasso Club




January 4th 2023


To inculcate entrepreneurial skill among youngsters of different streams, MANASSO CLUB of Takshashila University conducts stall activity BAZAAR 1.0 on 04.01.2024 from 9:30 am to 4:30 pm. 15 vibrant stalls featuring a variety of offerings from tempting food stalls to engaging game stalls has been erected. Also special stalls with refreshing juices, cotton candy, baked items, confectionery and smoke biscuits have been erected.
20stuent coordinators from MBA coordinating the event. Based on highest profit, entertainment, creativity and innovative approach, the winners will be selected for TU BAZAAR trophy. Also, the runners will be determined by the high-profit-making teams adding a competitive edge to the event.
